About the Brand

Winona’s Indie Bookshop is a hub for marginalized indie writers to share their work with new readers.
The brand started under the name Interviews with Indie Authors. After one season, the company rebranded to Winona’s Indie Bookshelf to consolidate the brand name, create a connection to the owner, and plan for the future.

Our Quest

It's Simple. We created a space for marginalized indie writers to easily connect their work with future readers.
