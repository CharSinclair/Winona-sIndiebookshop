    Winona's Indie Bookshop

    Our Brand

    Winona's Indie bookshop is a grassroots project to create a hub for indie authors to promote their work. We are currently in the community building phase of the project to gain online and local (Vegas) support. This is a space created by and for book lovers left to the margins looking to support one an other. We often see BIPOC, LGBTQIA+, and disabled people turn to independent forms of publishing due to the continued systemic oppression of marginalized people. Our quest is simple. Create spaces for marginalized indie writers to easily connect their work with future readers through community building events, an online community, and a youtube podcast. We create community unity through our book community.
